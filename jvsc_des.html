Japanese Vehicle Service Centre App

Japanese Vehicle Service Centre App is an android application that helps the company manaage their finances and gives statistical data on clients received and services rendered over a given period of time.

The features of this app includes:

  1. Daily record data & reports - Here the user can generate daily reports from the app showing the business performance.
  2. Vehicle Bookings - This feature allows the user to book client vehicles as the app simultaneously records the services rendered to generate a monthly and yearly report.
  3. Monthly & yearly performance data - The app takes all the data it gets from the user and generates performance data from the given input over a period of time.
  4. Acts as a company dashboard. - Any important news/information that needs to be received by employees and members of JVSC can be posted on the app by the admin and it will be broadcasted to all other devices.
  5. Performance graphs. - Monthly and yearly performance graphs are also available to help compare the data with ease.
